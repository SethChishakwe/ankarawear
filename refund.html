Refund & Return Policy

Due to the handcrafted and highly individualized nature of our garments, our return policy varies depending on the type of purchase.

Bespoke & Custom Orders: Because these items are tailored to your exact measurements and specifications, all custom orders are final sale and cannot be returned or refunded. If there is a structural flaw or an error on our part, we will offer complimentary alterations.

Ready-to-Wear Items: Standard store items in unworn, unwashed condition with all original tags attached may be returned for a refund or exchanged within 14 days of receipt. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.

To initiate a return, please reach out to our team via the Contact Us page with your order number.
